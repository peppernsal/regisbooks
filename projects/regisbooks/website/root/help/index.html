Help & Documentation

Browse all available book listings. You can filter, sort, and view details for each listing.

  • Navigate to View Listings from the sidebar.
  • Use the search and filter options to find specific books.
  • Click on a listing to see more details.

Add a new book listing by entering its ISBN or scanning its barcode.

  • Select Add Listing from the sidebar.
  • Enter the ISBN and click the button OR scan the barcode of your book.
  • You will be redirected to the Add Listing Info page.

Add or edit a book listing by entering pickup info and details.

  • You will reach this page after entering a Book's ISBN from the Add Listing page in the sidebar.
  • If you are editing the listing, you will reach this page after clicking "Edit this Listing" from a listing's page
  • Select the book's usage level, add notes, and specify at least one pickup location.
  • Avoid listing books that have medium to high annotation levels (especially for annotation-heavy courses like English). Light annotations for other courses are alright, but please specify if this is the case in the "Notes" section.
  • Submit the form to post your listing.

View and your profile information and your own listings.

  • Go to Profile from the sidebar.
  • View your personal information, stats, and unclaimed posted listings.
  • You can also navigate to other users' profiles from the Leaderboard page or a listing's details page

Browse available and requested listings.

  • Navigate to View Listings from the sidebar.
  • You can use the filters to narrow down your search.
  • Ten listings are shown per page. You can use the buttons at the bottom of the View Listings page to load more pages of listings

Open a request to claim an available book.

  • Navigate to View Listings from the sidebar.
  • Click on the listing you would like to request.
  • Click on the "Request This Listing" button to open your request.
  • Use the lister's email to communicate book exchange logistics.
  • The lister can mark the exchange as complete once you receive the book.

Your incoming and outgoing requests are summarized here.

  • Click Manage Requests in the sidebar to view your requests.
  • Incoming requests are from users who requested your listings.
  • Outgoing requests are sent by you whenever you request another user's listing.
  • Clicking the "Open a New Request" button will show you all available (unrequested) listings.

Users are ranked by their Aura on the leaderboard.

  • Click Leaderboard in the sidebar to view the ten users with the highest Aura.
  • Users get +1 Aura for every listing created.
  • Users get +3 Aura for every book given away.

Log out of your account.

  • Click Logout in the sidebar to end your session.
