About RegisBooks

The RegisBooks project aims to increase the circulation and re-use of student textbooks within Regis. The project enables students to easily lend and give physical copies of books to each other, thereby reducing the amount of money that is spent on buying textbooks each year and reducing the number of textbooks that inevitably go out of use early. Students can create accounts and post their books from the completed academic year so that underclassmen can request and claim them. For instructions on how to use the site, please see Help & Documentation.

This project is not officially affiliated with Regis at the moment. It is currently maintained by Carl Furtado '26.
